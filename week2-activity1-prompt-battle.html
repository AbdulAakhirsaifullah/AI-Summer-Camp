Prompt Battle

Work with your partner to create the best AI prompt possible. Every pair receives the same challenge. Your goal is to write one shared prompt that produces the strongest AI response. Afterward, the class will compare results and vote for the winning prompt.

Your Challenge

Create Your Prompt

Work together to write one clear and effective prompt. Think carefully about the audience, the goal, and how you want the AI to respond.

🤝 Your Mission

Work together to create one clear and effective AI prompt. Discuss different ideas before agreeing on your final version.

  • Read the challenge carefully.
  • Discuss different prompt ideas with your partner.
  • Agree on one shared prompt.
  • Run your prompt using an AI chatbot.

Share and Vote

  • Return to the main class.

  • Vote for the prompt that produced the best result.
  • Notice what made the winning prompt more effective.
