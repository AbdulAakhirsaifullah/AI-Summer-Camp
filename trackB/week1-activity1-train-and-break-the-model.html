Before you begin

In this activity, you will train your own AI gesture classifier using Google Teachable Machine. Then, you'll become an AI investigator by designing your own experiments to see when your model succeeds and when it fails.

Step 1 — Train Your AI

  • 1 Open Google Teachable Machine
  • 2 Create 3 gesture classes
  • 3 Capture approximately 40 webcam images for each gesture
  • 4 Train your model
  • 5 Test that all three gestures are recognized correctly before moving on
Open Teachable Machine ↗

Step 2 — Challenge Your AI

💡 Think about what's different between your training photos and a brand new situation.

Design three different experiments to try to confuse your own AI model. Be creative and investigate what affects your model's performance.

🧪 Your Mission

Design three different experiments to try to confuse your own AI model.

  • 1Design a new test.
  • 2Run the test using your trained model.
  • 3Observe how your AI responds.
  • 4Think about why your model behaved that way.
💡 Be creative! Design your own experiments instead of copying someone else's ideas.

Step 3 — What Did You Learn?

Think about everything you discovered during your experiments.
