🎯 Your Mission

In this activity, you'll compete against AI in four quick challenges. For each round:

  • Complete the task yourself first.
  • Watch the teacher test the same challenge using AI.
  • Compare your answer with the AI's.
  • Think about who performed better, and why.
👤 No teams — everyone participates individually.
Round 1 · Word Prediction

🔤 Word Prediction

The teacher will display an incomplete sentence, for example:

"Yesterday I forgot my ______."

Write the word you think comes next. Do NOT use AI — think on your own first.

Round 2 · Image Captioning

🖼️ Image Captioning

The teacher will display a funny or unusual picture. Write your own caption before seeing the AI version.

Round 3 · Quick Draw Reverse

🎨 Quick Draw Reverse

The teacher will tell you an abstract idea to draw — for example: justice, love, freedom, disappointment. Use Quick Draw on your own computer and see if the AI recognizes your drawing.

Round 4 · Fake or Real?

🧠 Fake or Real?

The teacher will display several short texts. For each one, decide: did a human write it, or an AI?

🎯 What We Learned

AI is incredibly fast and powerful at many tasks, but humans still have strengths in creativity, judgment, emotion, and understanding context. The best results come when humans and AI work together.
