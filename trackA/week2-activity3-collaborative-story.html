📖 Collaborative Silly Story

Track A · Week 2 · Activity 3 — The whole class builds one silly story together

15 min · whole class

Let's write a story with AI — together!

One person starts. AI writes a bit. The class votes on what happens next. We do this a few times until we have a whole silly story!

🎬 How it works

💡
Step 1
Someone shares a silly idea

A talking sandwich? A dragon scared of ladders? Any silly idea can start the story!

⬇️
🔊
Step 2
We read what AI wrote out loud

The teacher types the idea into AI, and the whole class listens to what happens.

⬇️
🙋
Step 3
The class votes on what happens next

A few people share ideas, then everyone votes with a show of hands for their favorite.

⬇️
✏️
Step 4
The teacher tells AI what we picked

AI keeps writing the story using the class's idea — then we go back to Step 2!

🔁 Repeat Steps 2–4 about 4–5 times, then read the whole story out loud!
